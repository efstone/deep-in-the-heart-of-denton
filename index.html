Our Mission

Deep in the Heart of Denton is a nonprofit after-school enrichment program that extends literacy beyond the classroom.

Our mission is to foster meaningful engagement with the city, help students build a mental map of downtown Denton, and nurture a lifelong love of reading.

We collaborate with school administrators, teachers, families, and local businesses and organizations to offer free, meaningful experiences that connect students to the heart of their city. This also helps local businesses and organizations get to know the students and families in their neighborhood and build lasting relationships that strengthen our entire community.

Our Background

Gary Barnhart and Ethan Bell created Deep in the Heart of Denton to give Title I students an opportunity to explore their city.

Each field trip brings a busload of third through fifth graders downtown for an afternoon that includes a StoryWalk® around the Square, a sit-down meal at a local restaurant, and time to visit the courthouse lawn. For many students, it is their first time experiencing downtown as a place that welcomes them.

By connecting students, families, and local businesses, the program helps strengthen the bonds that hold the Denton community together.

Our Programs

Downtown Denton StoryWalk®

Deep in the Heart of Denton has partnered with the Denton Public Library to celebrate literacy and community connection through Denton's StoryWalk® experience. Our team created a user-friendly map and worksheet to accompany the StoryWalk® books curated each quarter by Haley Phillips, Outreach Librarian for Denton Public Library.

Printed copies of the map and worksheet are available at all three branches of the Denton Public Library as well as the Discover Denton Visitor Center, where the StoryWalk® begins.

This initiative supports Deep in the Heart of Denton's mission to engage students on after school field trips and connect them with the people, stories, and landmarks of their downtown community.
