Our Mission

Deep in the Heart of Denton is an after-school enrichment program that extends literacy beyond the classroom.

Our mission is to foster meaningful engagement with the city, help students build a mental map of downtown Denton, and nurture a lifelong love of reading.

We collaborate with school administrators, teachers, families, and local businesses and organizations to offer free, meaningful experiences that connect students to the heart of their city. This also helps local businesses and organizations get to know the students and families in their neighborhood and build lasting relationships that strengthen our entire community.

Our Programs

Downtown Discovery

Students explore downtown Denton through guided field trips, building a mental map of their community while visiting local businesses, museums, and cultural sites.

Reading Adventures

Interactive reading sessions that bring stories to life through visits to relevant locations, connecting literature to real-world experiences in our community.

Community Connections

Building lasting relationships between students, families, and local organizations through collaborative events and meaningful interactions.
